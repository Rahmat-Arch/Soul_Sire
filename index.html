Grail Knight

Welcome to Grail Knight, an epic adventure game where you take on the role of a brave knight on a quest to save your kingdom. Explore vast dungeons, battle fearsome monsters, and uncover hidden treasures. Every step brings you closer to defeating the dark demon lord who threatens your world.

Use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to move, and press the attack button to swing your sword. You'll need wit and courage to overcome traps, solve puzzles, and upgrade your gear along the way.
